# RegReporting Compass

A cross-jurisdiction derivatives reporting comparison tool 
covering EMIR REFIT, MiFID II, CFTC Part 45, HKMA, and MAS.

## What it covers

- Product eligibility across 6 asset classes
- 10 key reporting attributes with field-level validation rules
- 4 annotated cross-border business scenarios  
- Counterparty classification and thresholds
- 6 major cross-regime interpretation differences

## How to use

**Online:** [Launch the tool](https://yourusername.github.io/regreporting-compass)

**Offline:** Download `index.html` and open in any browser. 
No internet connection required after download.

## Data sources

- ESMA EMIR REFIT Final RTS (effective Apr 29, 2024)
- CFTC 17 CFR Part 45 Technical Specification (effective Dec 5, 2022)
- MiFID II RTS 22 — Commission Delegated Regulation 2017/590
- HKMA SFO Schedule 10A Reporting Rules
- MAS Notice SFR-N01 / SFA04-N02
